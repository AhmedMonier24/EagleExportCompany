Our Services

We Ship on Time

EAGLE EXPORT company has many branches, HQ is in Egypt and the other branch office in Russia. We specialize mainly in exports of agricultural products. Our core business focuses on trading only the best quality fruits and vegetables, while maintaining the highest possible level of sorting, packaging and quality control processes.

We are dedicated to offering our customers with any other kind of A Class agricultural product upon their request, while supplying all year round our international clients Wholesalers, Distributors or Retailers.

EAGLE EXPORT has a good market footprint and very potential customers in Europe, Gulf Area, Africa and Fareast Region …….

Our wide variety of Fresh Vegetables, Fruits, are cultivated in controlled environment by using advanced technology and harmless fertilizers. Furthermore, our Fresh Vegetables and Fruits can be availed at the market leading prices by the clients.

Introducing innovative ideas in sourcing and exporting, we have managed to improve speed, efficiency and on time delivery. All our operations are supported by a talented, hardworking human resource. With a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction, we have been offering world class products at the most competitive prices.
